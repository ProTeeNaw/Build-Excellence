Environmental and Occupational Safety

Build Excellence follows all the laid down mutational statutes and city by-laws in the development of our clientâ€™s properties. The company undertakes only the projects that have been approved while it also carries out its own environmental impact assessments for each individual project.

Each working environment is supposed to follow the national safety standards in Chapter 8, Act 85, which gives workers the rights to health and safety in the workplace. The regulations in the Act gives guidelines on things like toilets, changerooms, first aid, drinking water, washing facilities, protective clothing, machinery, staking and packing, ladders, fire, ventilation, lighting, temperature, noise and asbestos safety.

Inspectors have wide powers in terms of the Act to make sure that the employer and workers follow the Act. Our management sets up safety representatives and safety committees to ensure that the workers have a say in their own occupational safety.
